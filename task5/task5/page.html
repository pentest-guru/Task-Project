This innovation strategy plan is more than just a guide for business success.

A business strategy plays a crucial role in defining the course of action for organisational objectives, helping businesses create a competitive edge, improve customer satisfaction, and stay relevant in the market. Business strategy facilitates smooth functioning across different levels of an organisation, namely corporate level strategy, business level strategy, and functional level strategy, thereby promoting cohesion and collaboration among employees. A comprehensive business strategy consists of five key components including business objective, core values, SWOT analysis, operational tactics, and measurement mechanics to track performance.

What is Business strategy?

A business strategy is a business document that describes a course of action to help leaders achieve organisational objectives. The detailed plan defines business needs to guide the hiring process and inform the allocation of company resources. It provides a clear direction for different teams to pool together their talents to support the company's goals. As a result, businesses secure a competitive position in the market, improve customer satisfaction and mobilise their business operations.
